    E-SMART HEALTH

    Health Care

    What Is E-SMART HEALTH ?

    With the e-smart healthcare Systems is a Medical Practice Management Software that deals with the day-to-day operations of a medical practice. This software allows users to capture patient demographics, schedule appointments, maintain lists of insurance payors, perform billing tasks, and generate reports

    The manager will be in a position to capture attendance, inventory, trend analysis, performance analysis with this application. The end user mobile app will be helpful for customer retention by way of discounts/royalty incentives etc.

    Know More

    APPOINMENT MANAGEMENT

    We can take Online appointment with doctor by sending a request from mobile app by specifying symptoms so doctor can suggest quick medicines to patient for quick relief.

    INSURANCE AND BILLING

    Using Smart Health app we can track and monitor, insurance status and billing activities also we can set reminder for upcoming billing cycle.

    PHARMACY MANGEMENT

    Using Smart Health app we have facility to upload prescriptions and other medical reports so we can manage pharmacy billing and management.

    REPORT CENTER

    Using Smart Health app we can manage different type of reports like doctor number of visit in particular month/year, total amount charge in a particular month/year so we can manage our health report.
